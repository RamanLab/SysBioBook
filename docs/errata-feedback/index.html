    Errata & Feedback

    Feedback #

    Despite my best efforts and those of my colleagues and students who have carefully gone through the book, it is likely that there may be a few typographical/grammatical errors. Do send your feedback via GitHub—critical feedback will be much appreciated and duly acknowledged!

    Errata #

    • p. 163, Chapter 7: First line below equation 7.3c: composes should read compose
    • p. 176, Chapter 8: In the footnote, CH3 should read CH₃
    • p. 278, Chapter 12: Elowitz and Liebler should read Elowitz and Leibler [ref.]. The reference is Elowitz, M., Leibler, S. A synthetic oscillatory network of transcriptional regulators. Nature 403:335–338 (2000)
