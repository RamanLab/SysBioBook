    Chapter 11

    Chapter 11: Modelling cellular interactions #

    Host–pathogen systems biology
    Forst (2006) Drug Discovery Today

    A nice review on host–pathogen systems biology.

    Modelling Viral and Immune System Dynamics
    Perelson (2002) Nature Reviews Immunology

    An authoritative review on modelling the dynamics of viral infections e.g. HIV/HCV.

    Immunology for physicists
    Perelson (1997) Reviews of Modern Physics

    A detailed review of an extraordinary variety of models of the immune system, from a physicist’s perspective.

    Boolean models of within-host immune interactions
    Thakar and Albert (2010) Current Opinion in Microbiology

    An excellent application of Boolean network modelling to study host–pathogen interactions.

    Constraint-based analysis of metabolic capacity of Salmonella typhimurium during host-pathogen interaction
    Raghunathan et al. (2009) BMC Systems Biology

    A nice application of constraint-based modelling to study host–pathogen interactions.

    A systems perspective of host–pathogen interactions: predicting disease outcome in tuberculosis
    Raman et al. (2010) Molecular BioSystems

    A detailed systems-level model of TB infection, capturing a large number of interactions between the pathogen and the host immune system.

    Current trends in modeling host–pathogen interactions
    Mukherjee et al. (2013) Wiley Interdisciplinary Reviews: Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery

    Another nice review on host–pathogen systems biology.
